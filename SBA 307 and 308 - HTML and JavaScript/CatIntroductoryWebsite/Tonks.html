Tonkerbelle

Government Name: Tonkerbelle
Nicknames: Tonks, Tonky-Wonky
Tonks is the sister to Tink. They were both adopted from a PetsMart in San Antonio after losing my long-time cat Whiskers. They're what I call the 'Originals' because they were the first adopted of my brood. Tonks is named in part after an SNL Skit Tonkerbelle, and also in part after Tonks from Harry Potter. Her favorite toy is a rattle mouse.
